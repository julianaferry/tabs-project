Directions

Set your maps to Manchester Airport and exit the M56 at Junction 5 and get in lane for Terminal 1 (T1). At the set of traffic, go straight ahead for T1.

At the roundabout, take the 5th exit and follow signs for T1 Meet & Greet(T1 M&G). After 0.1 mile, take the first available left turn and continue 0.1 mile to a roundabout. Take the first exit and the entrance to T1 M&G is then immediately on your right.
